Delivery Orders

Delivery orders are accepted if the following criteria are met:

  • The customer's delivery location falls within the permitted delivery zone of the designated Knight Bite outlet.
  • The delivery address being mapped to the outlet that delivers in the customer's delivery location.
  • Online availability of the outlet to accept and process the order.
  • If the customer's delivery location is not listed within the permitted delivery zone, the order cannot be placed. Pickup may be chosen from the permitted zone.

Menu

  • The menu is displayed as per the availability of the menu items in the outlet.
  • If ordered menu items are unavailable, the order will not be executed and the customer will be informed.

Terms & Conditions for Coupons

  • Coupons cannot be combined with other offers.

Modify / Cancel Order

  • Orders once placed cannot be modified or cancelled.
  • Order progress can be checked by contacting the outlet.
  • Refunds for cancelled card payments will reflect in the next billing cycle.
  • Food is for immediate consumption (within 45 minutes).

Refund

Refunds are processed after review by Knight Bite customer care for valid reasons.
